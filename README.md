# ReliantFlow: AI-Powered Contract & Compliance Sovereignty 
AI-native contract intelligence for global creators and SME's, providing automated risk assessments and compliance sovereignty. 

##The Mission
Independent creators and service-based SMEs lose an estimated **15%% of their revenue to "compliance friction" -the inability to effectively audit complex contracts or navigate cross-border tax requirements. 

**ReliantFlow** is an agentic, secure-by-design platform that democratizes enterprise-grade contract-intelligence, ensuring that every independent professional can operate with the same riger and protection as a global corporation. 
-----
## THe Problem
Current tools are either too expensive or require legal expertise that small businesses lack. This leads to: 
* **Predatory Contracting:** Creators unknowingly sign agreements with hidden liabilities, rights-grabs, or unfair royalty splits.
*  **Compliance Negligence:** SMEs struggle with cross-border tax forms (eg, W-8BEN, VAT/GST) and reconciliaton, leading to delayed paymentsand audit risks
  ---

  ##Our Solution: The "COOin the Pocket"
  ReliantFlow bridges the gap between the signing of a contract and the realization of revenue through: 

  * **Dual-Engine Intelligence:** Specialized AI agents trained to flag risks specifically for **Publishing/Royalty Agreements** or **General B2B Service Contracts**.
  * **Secure-by-Design Privacy:** We implement **PII masking** (Personal Identifiable Information) *before* ingestion, ensuring sensitive data remains private.
  * ** Agentic Risk Reporting:** We provide verifiable, strucvtured "Intelligence Notes" that prioritize risks by severity and offer legally sound, alternative clause language.
